This is my idea guys: A platform where you must give away your items within a set amount of time (example 2hours), or else they will be donated to charity.
 
Problem: Consumerism creates bad hoarding habits. People always want to give away stuff but yet procrastinate and action will never be taken. Prime example of myself being hesitant to give away clothes that I can no longer fit in, in the name of "it holds sentimental value" so it has been tucked away in a vacuum sealed box in the corner of the storage room. So, if money makes our decision of buying items so easy, we can apply that to giving away things as well!
 
Solution: We cultivate a sense of URGENCY (people always rush to buy things when promotions last for a limited time only). List the item, and you have to give it away in 2 hours. Else, it will automatically be given to charity (reducing consumerism in everyday life). That way its a win for all sides: The individual reduced consumerism, receiving party reduces materialism as well!
 
Selling point of the product: "2 hours! No demand from the majority of people, the charities could make good use of it."
